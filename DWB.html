About

Who

Doctors Without Borders travels all around the world helping people in countries that don't have access to the medicine and care they need.

What

Foreign aid is a social cause everyone needs to be aware of. The first step to changing the world is being aware. We want to raise awareness for foreign aid to help you take the first step in making a difference in the lives of people who need your help.

Where

All around the world disaster and disease are effecting the health of people. Doctors without borders goes to so many countries such as China, India, and Nepal. To see all of them click here

Why

Imagine your life is great. You have a job, family, good health, a home. But one day you were exposed to a deadly disease or infection and were going to die in a year or less. You separate yourself from your family so they don't suffer as you do. The pain is excruciating and you can't stand up to work. There was nothing you could do. No medicine. No pain killers. No doctor. Just waiting to die.

Now imagine hearing about doctors helping people around the world. You have hope. You find where they are in your area and you get treated with the medicine you need to survive. You can now go back to your family and and hug them. No more pain and isolation. You have your life back.

This is why.
